#<S&S Lite>

    #<Description>

        By Aaron0000

        S&S LITE

        Completely overhauls the Scav manufacturer into S&S from Borderlands 1.

        DIFFERENCES BETWEEN THIS AND S&S FOREVER;

        Does not have the ammo drain mechanic of S&S Forever.

        Weapons cost ammo like normal but slowly regenerate ammo over time.

        No changes made to some of the accessories.

        List of Changes;

        Most Scav items (excluding class mods/oz kits and some unique/legendary items) have been reskinned and renamed. Names and prefixes are based off of space objects and the like. Only "Scav" thing that remains is the logo in the item cards.

        S&S Weapons have been globally rebalanced. The stats should be fairly balanced in all aspects (minus a penalty in fire rate). S&S weapons also have a 25% increased chance to inflict a status effect compared to other non-Maliwan manufacturers.

        Several Uniques Weapons and Legendary Weapons have been replaced or adjusted. Details down below.

        If using the community patch, load this file after the community patch. In general, load this file last.

        NOTES:

        Do not use this with S&S Forever.

    #</Description>

                Significantly increased elemental damage.

            #</Cassiopeia>

            #<Orion>

                Fires one round that divides into three upon impact with a surface.

                Spawns in Shock only (but will not delete non-elemental versions).

                Increased Accuracy.

                Increased elemental chance.

            #</Orion>

            #<Draco>

                Dual-element weapon with large splash radius. Incendiary and Explosive.

                Boosted damage and fire rate.

                Fires in the shape of the constellation Draco.

            #</Draco>

            #<Thanatos>

                Increased melee damage.

                Increased movement speed.

                Reduces Action Skill Cooldown time when held.

            #</Thanatos>

                Damage dealt is always explosive, regardless of weapon card information.
