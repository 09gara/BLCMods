Takes all of the bad class mods (Excluding the legendaries) and overhauls them all, making them better than before!

[Notes]

Due to gearbox brilliance, Aurelia's 'Master' COM can't have a secondary stat bonus, as far as i know.
Some of the COMs have secret boosts (Hard-To-Fix UI issues which makes them invisible) which are listed in the [Changes].
Work In Progress. Version 1 is bound to improve.
Class mods can be reverted in newer versions if the community votes to make me do so.

[Stat Changes]

Everything before the > is old and everything after the > is what it is changed to.
