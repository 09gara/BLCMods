Turns the tediore barrels on all shotguns into a carnage barrel; the old classic carnage from Borderlands 1 is back!

Fully compatible with the community patch.

[Note]

Rockets can still hurt you (obviously)
This mod affects enemy shotguns as well.
Reload speed and fire rate might be a little bit too low.

>>EXECUTE THIS PATCH AFTER ALL OTHER PATCHES YOU HAVE FOR BEST RESULTS.<<

[Features]

- Very high damage boost (basically it's the damage of when every pellet would normally hit, plus a little more.)
- Nerfed reload speed and accuracy.
- Hyperion and Jakobs weapons have a majorly nerfed fire rate to balance it with other weapons.
- Hyperion and Bandit weapons have a slightly nerfed clip size to balance it with other weapons.
- All manufacturers now have a more balanced out fire rate to balance it with each other manufacturer.

+ Works with elemental types for elemental boom!

>> Old Hyperion shotguns, unlike regular Hyperion shotguns, have slower reload speed, equal fire rate, more clip size and damage, and a bit less accuracy.

[Changelog]

V2.0 Fixed vertical grip to never add projectiles on a carnage weapon, but still adds them on other weapons.
13th june 2017 ported to Borderlands 2.
