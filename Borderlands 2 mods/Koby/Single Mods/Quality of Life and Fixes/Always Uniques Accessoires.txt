    By Koby

    Makes all Unique / Aftermarket weapons always spawn with an accessory.

    Goes wonderfully with my "Double Quest Rewards" mod.

    Fully modular with the Filter Tool.

    This doesn't affect existing weapons.

    This doesn't violate sanity check.

    Should probably load before any weapon mod and let the weapon mods overwrite this one.
