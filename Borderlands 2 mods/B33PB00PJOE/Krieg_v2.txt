#<Krieg>

    Mod by B33P B00P JOE

    *************

    This overhaul is meant to take skills that were often either overlooked or outright "bad" with Krieg

    and spice them up, or give them at least some well earned polish!

    Included are the community patch buffs to Krieg as of 3.1

    As well as a collection of fixes by TheNocturni and Soze atop my own input.

    They have a few other things for Krieg on their GitHubs that I did not include, but feel free to add them to the pack!

    *************

    #<Change List>

        *************

        Changelist:

        *************

        Blood Euphoria Added [UCP]

        Raving Retribution Rants [UCP]

        Salt the Wound Icon Stacking Fix [UCP]

        Silence the Voices made to not be Grenade Damage [UCP]

        Silence the Voices visual clarity [Soze]

        Blood Twitch Passive instead of Kill Skill [Nocturni]

        Flame Flare Burn Resistance [Nocturni]

        Kill Skill Slots Fix [Nocturni]

        *************

        -----------------------------

        Bloody Revival:

        -----------------------------

        - Damage per stack 1.5 --> 1.0

        + Affects Gun, Melee and Grenade Damage in FFYL

        *************

        -----------------------------

        Pull the Pin:

        -----------------------------

        - Made Tier 2

        + Grenade Damage boost is now 15% from 10% in the UCP. 

        *************

        -----------------------------

        Embrace the Pain:

        -----------------------------

        - Reduced max fire rate boost to 25%

        + Added 25% Elemental DoT resistance when active

        + Works during Buzz Axe Ramapage / FFYL

        *************

        -----------------------------

        Fuel the Rampage:

        -----------------------------

        - Friendly Fire removed

        + Replaced with 25% movement speed at 5/5

        + This movement speed also increases as you take Health Damage.

        + Movement speed applies in FFYL

        *************

        -----------------------------

        Light the Fuse:

        -----------------------------

        + Thrown Dynamite Sticks have increased damage and blast radius.

        + Affected by Bloody Revival

        + Does a bit more damage overall

        *************

        -----------------------------

        Thrill of the Kill:

        -----------------------------

        * Swapped places with Salt the Wound, is now a tier 4  skill while StW is a Tier 3.

        + Overkill healing is now 100% at 5/5

        + Works during FFYL, but at a -vastly- reduced rate

        + Works well combined with Light the Fuse as you can explode a ton of trash mobs for a revival boost.

        + Also increases Max HP by up to 35% at 5/5

        *************

        -----------------------------

        Hellfire Halitosis:

        -----------------------------

        + Damage and Range Tripled

        - Cooldown increased from 15 sec to 20 sec

        + Counts as Melee Damage

        *************

        _____________

        *************

        Includes TPS skills compatability for Krieg!

        *************

    #</Change List>

*************

This overhaul is meant to take skills that were often either overlooked or outright "bad" with Krieg

and spice them up, or give them at least some well earned polish!

Included are the community patch buffs to Krieg as of 3.1

As well as a collection of fixes by TheNocturni and Soze atop my own input.

They have a few other things for Krieg on their GitHubs that I did not include, but feel free to add them to the pack!

Changelist:

*************

Blood Euphoria Added [UCP]

Raving Retribution Rants [UCP]

Salt the Wound Icon Stacking Fix [UCP]

Silence the Voices made to not be Grenade Damage [UCP]

Silence the Voices visual clarity [Soze]

Blood Twitch Passive instead of Kill Skill [Nocturni]

Flame Flare Burn Resistance [Nocturni]

Kill Skill Slots Fix [Nocturni]

*************

-----------------------------

Bloody Revival:

-----------------------------

- Damage per stack 1.5 --> 1.0

+ Affects Gun, Melee and Grenade Damage in FFYL

*************

-----------------------------

Pull the Pin:

-----------------------------

- Made Tier 2

+ Grenade Damage boost is now 15% from 10% in the UCP. 

*************

-----------------------------

Embrace the Pain:

-----------------------------

- Reduced max fire rate boost to 25%

+ Added 25% Elemental DoT resistance when active

+ Works during Buzz Axe Ramapage / FFYL

*************

-----------------------------

Fuel the Rampage:

-----------------------------

- Friendly Fire removed

+ Replaced with 25% movement speed at 5/5

+ This movement speed also increases as you take Health Damage.

+ Movement speed applies in FFYL

*************

-----------------------------

Light the Fuse:

-----------------------------

+ Thrown Dynamite Sticks have increased damage and blast radius.

+ Affected by Bloody Revival

+ Does a bit more damage overall

*************

-----------------------------

Thrill of the Kill:

-----------------------------

* Swapped places with Salt the Wound, is now a tier 4  skill while StW is a Tier 3.

+ Overkill healing is now 100% at 5/5

+ Works during FFYL, but at a -vastly- reduced rate

+ Works well combined with Light the Fuse as you can explode a ton of trash mobs for a revival boost.

+ Also increases Max HP by up to 35% at 5/5

*************

-----------------------------

Hellfire Halitosis:

-----------------------------

+ Damage and Range Tripled

- Cooldown increased from 15 sec to 20 sec

+ Counts as Melee Damage
