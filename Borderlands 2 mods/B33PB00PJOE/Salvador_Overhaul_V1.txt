#<Salvador>

    Mod by B33P B00P JOE

    *************

    This overhaul is meant to take Sal in a fresh, new direction in many areas.

    Focusing on alternative playstyles, as well as ironing out "questionable" attributes,

    the Salvadore_Overhaul offers many more skill synergies throughout each of the skill trees

    instead of mainly just Gun Lust and Rampage. 

    Many underperforming skills have been brought up to par, including replacing

    "All I Need is One" with a new skill: "Hot Load".

    Each skill below has a description of changes, as well as design notes where applicable.

    *************

    #<Gunzerking Tweaks>

        #<Description>

            *************

            Gunzerking Changes:

            Damage Reduction: 0.5 --> 0.6

            Ammo Regeneration: 3 --> 1.5

            Weapon Swap Speed: 0% --> +50%

            Weapon Damage: 0% --> -25%

            Weapon Accuracy: 0% --> -25%

            Melee Override Cooldown: 0% --> +100% (Halved)

            ___________________________________________

            These changes are a starting point to branch out into different build possibilities. 

            In the trees, various skills were altered to grant increased perks that with vanilla gunzerking, may be a tad overwhelming. 

            Melee Override and Damage Reduction offer better melee / survival as a possible route

            Swap Speed is a nice general boost and synergizes greatly with overlooked Gun Lust skills

            The Ammo regen nerf is offset by the inclusion of Ammo Regen in skills that can be always on, and are even boostable.

            The damage and accuracy tweaks are offset by the fact that he gains more damage boosts in his skills now, and there are hidden recoil buffs in Gunzerk already.

            *************

        #</Description>

Mod by B33P B00P JOE

*************

This overhaul is meant to take Sal in a fresh, new direction in many areas.

Focusing on alternative playstyles, as well as ironing out "questionable" attributes, 

the Salvadore_Overhaul offers many more skill synergies throughout each of the skill trees 

instead of mainly just Gun Lust and Rampage. 

Many underperforming skills have been brought up to par, including replacing 

"All I Need is One" with a new skill: "Hot Load".

Each skill below has a description of changes, as well as design notes where applicable.

*************

*************

Gunzerking Changes:

Damage Reduction: 0.5 --> 0.6

Ammo Regeneration: 3 --> 1.5

Weapon Swap Speed: 0% --> +50%

Weapon Damage: 0% --> -25%

Weapon Accuracy: 0% --> -25%

Melee Override Cooldown: 0% --> +100% (Halved)

___________________________________________

These changes are a starting point to branch out into different build possibilities. 

In the trees, various skills were altered to grant increased perks that with vanilla gunzerking, may be a tad overwhelming. 

Melee Override and Damage Reduction offer better melee / survival as a possible route

Swap Speed is a nice general boost and synergizes greatly with overlooked Gun Lust skills

The Ammo regen nerf is offset by the inclusion of Ammo Regen in skills that can be always on, and are even boostable.

The damage and accuracy tweaks are offset by the fact that he gains more damage boosts in his skills now, and there are hidden recoil buffs in Gunzerk already.

*************

*************

Quickdraw:

Swap Speed: +40% max --> +50% max

Re-Equip/Un-Equip time: 0% --> +25% max

Removed Critical Hit Bonus

__________________________________

Focusing just on swap speed attributes helps greatly both in general gameplay,

But also within the Gun Lust tree with synergies between "Auto Loader" and 

"Hot Load".

*************

*************

I'm your Hucklberry:

Added bonuses for the offhand Weapon

Damage/Reload: 15% max --> 17.5% max

________________________________________

UCP changes, + a little extra specialization given the Gunzerk tweak.

*************

*************

"Hot Load" (All I Need is One):

Completely reworked into a new skill = "Hot Load"

Swapping weapons "refreshes" them, granting a 5.5-second boost to damage. (Same duration as Locked and Loaded)

This damage bonus scales up to +55%, based on ammo currently in the magazine:

25/50 bullets = 22.5% damage boost, and so on. 

Swapping weapons refreshes this bonus, and it synergizes well 

with skills that reload fast, or keep you at high ammo.

_____________________________________________________________

As "All I Need is One" was extremely lackluster, creating a 

new skill that has the same spirit seemed appropriate. 

This now boasts great synergy with Auto Loader, Quick Draw,

and the new Inconceivable in particular.

*************

*************

Money Shot:

Damage Type: MT_Scale --> MT_PostAdd (Multiplier to Additive after everything else)

Min Clip size for Bonus: 10 --> 8

___________________________________

Bit of a toss up, more guns can now use money shot effectively,

but the Damage Type change keeps it from being as wacky. 

The big change here is of course the essential removal of Money Shot chains.

*************

*************

Auto Loader:

Grants a passive 15% Reload Speed Buff

________________________________________

UCP Change, makes the game changer a bit more special and synergize with Locked & loaded

*************

*************

Lay Waste:

Added bonuses for the Offhand weapon

Additionally regenerates ammo while active, max of 1.0 / 0.025 for normal/rocket ammo.

_______________________________________________________________________________

UCP change, as well as the addition of Ammo Regen to supplement builds that may 

benefit more from avoiding the likes of Inconceivable for faster ammo expenditure.

*************

*************

Keep it Piping Hot:

Added bonuses to the offhand weapon

Gun Damage: 25% max --> 30% max

Grenade Damage: 25% max --> 30% max

____________________________________

Ironed out this skill to be appropriate for a tier 5

*************

*************

No Kill Like Overkill:

Duration: 10 sec --> 3 sec

Damage Scale: 100% --> 50%

Damage Type: MT_PostAdd --> MT_PreAdd (can receive damage bonuses from skills!)

Damage Scaling on kills has also been reversed where killing 

big targets now grants a superior bonus vs killing small targets.

_________________________________________________________________

In the vanilla game, this skill arguably was what pushed Sal over the egde.

The change to a 3 second duration, as well as the flip on who you'd want to aim to kill first 

adds a new layer of skill and strategy, while the change to PreAdd damage bonus allows you to 

make the most of the very brief window!

*************

*************

Makes I'm Ready Already Tier 1

Makes Inconceivable Tier 3

____________________________

This simultaneously buffs Ready Already by making it available to more builds,

and puts Inconceivable in a spot more worthy of it's power.

*************

*************
