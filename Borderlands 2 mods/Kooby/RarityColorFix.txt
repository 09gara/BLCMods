#<Rarity Colors Fix>

    #<Description>

        #NoMoreJebaited

        By Kooby

        Vanilla: https://i.imgur.com/15e0qIR.jpg

        RarityColorFix: https://i.imgur.com/dB3sFHz.jpg

        Changes the rarity color of unique aftermarket guns to a darker blue to be more distinguishable from normal rare guns.

        Also changes the color of Moxxi weapons to a blood red.

        Supports Gemstone weapons from the Unofficial Community Patch.

        Changes the order of weapons in the inventory.

        Pearl -> Seraph -> Legendary -> Moxxi -> Unique -> Gemstone -> E-Tech -> Very Rare...

        Full Filter Tool support!

        Thanks to Dave for providing the Rarity Colors resource.

        DISCLAIMER:

        This mod will most likely conflict with any mod which changes rarity colors.

        Shields, Grenade Mods and Relics aren't included since most of them don't have an unique material to them.

    #</Description>
