		Summary:
		
		- Replaces the Scorpio with the Widowmaker assault rifle
		
		- Moxxi weapon which heals for 4 % of the damage inflicted
		
		- Burst fire like the Scorpio

		- High elemental effect chance and damage over time
		
		- Massively increased melee damage
		
		- Bayonet only by default (also changes the look of the bayonet to the Bandit version. WARNING! This applies to ALL AR bayonets!)
		
		- Optional: Bayonet on slot 2, re-enables the other accessories
