Makes Captain Blade's Midnight Star a lot better than it previously was. It has a better gimmick than before!

[Features]
- New gimmick: ~ Very high damage, but also a very high blast radius, meaning you could hit yourself. The blast radius is the size between the tunguska and the nukem.
- New name and description.

Future update: elemental unlocks. (Not good with sanity checks.)

[Notes]

Works best with the community patch.

[Changelog]

V4.0 Lazy txt fixes
V3.0 New fix that prevents any child grenades from spawning on the Shipwreck grenade.
V2.5 Made the explosion hit through walls, better for enemies but not you. also one part on the grenade gives it 1 child grenade now..
V2.0 Multiple changes, including red text and curse text changes (thank you The_Nocturni)
     Buffed damage by quite alot, lowered blast radius
