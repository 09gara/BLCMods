Turns the Conferrence Call into the Black Death. A beautiful weapon that will alert the entire map once you shoot it.

[Notes]

Breaks the FiringModes of the Conferrence Call, Striker, Emperor and Octo. If you have no mods that use these firingModes, you can still use the weapons.
Beware of performance issues (lag) when having increased fire rate of this weapon.
Because of frame drops, do not use this on a low spec computer.

[Features]

- Always 7 projectiles.
- Bullets ricochet and split 5 times, and splitting goes as followed:
  The first ricochet splits into 5 at a 90 degree angle, the other 4 split into 1 bullet, at a 0 degree angle.

- Boosted crit damage and base damage.
- Very low fire rate (for a hyperion shotgun, might be fast compared to a bandit shotgun)
- High accuracy.
- Decent Clip size and Reload speed.
- Bullets pierce through enemies.
