        Turns the tediore barrels on shotguns into a carnage barrel; the old classic carnage from Borderlands 1 is back!

        You NEED to use this with the community patch if you want the pearlescent to work.

        Ported from The Pre-Sequel to Borderlands 2!

        #<Notes>

            Rockets can still hurt you, and this makes it so enemies can have a carnage too.

            Gives the KerBlaster a Firing Pattern. Use with caution.

            Execute the community patch, then this mod.

        #</Notes>

        #<Features>

            - Very high damage boost (basically it's the damage of when every pellet would normally hit, plus more.)

            - Nerfed reload speed and accuracy.

            - Hyperion and Jakobs weapons have a majorly nerfed fire rate to balance it with other weapons.

            - All manufacturers now have a more balanced out fire rate to balance it with each other manufacturer.

            - Works with elemental types for elemental boom!

            The patch comes with an improvement for the Pearlescent Carnage shotgun. >>IF YOU HAVE A MOD THAT REPLACES THIS WEAPON YOU CAN DISABLE IT.<<

            The carnage Pearlescent also has a shiny skin!

        #</Features>

        #<Changelog>
            
            V5.0 Changed FiringMode of the BFC. If you have a mod that uses the KerBlaster, this will break.

            Gave the BFC a little more shiny skin. (Very minor tweak.)
             
            V4.0 Nerfed damage, added pearlescent carnage change.

            Due to a bug with changing the pearl carnage firingmode, the tediore barreled carnage shotguns now use the Tediore launcher firingmode.

            V3.0 Fixed vertical grip to never add projectiles on a carnage weapon, but still adds them on other weapons.

            V2.0 Increased ammo consumption to 2 per shot, increased clip size to help compensate.

            13th of June 2017 Release + Ported over to Borderlands 2.

            The port includes changes such as:

            - Slightly better overall stats

            - Increased damage to compensate for OP levels. Still needs a good build in OP8 to be useful. (Ex. Deputy Sal)

        #</Changelog>

    #</Description>
