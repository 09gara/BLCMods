This mod makes it so that all the Vladof legendaries have infinite ammo, along with some additional changes. Includes balancing.

All these weapons are now usable on Gaige as well, due to 2 clip size instead of 1. Keep in mind the infinity might still not be usefull on her.

    #<Features>

(Endless) Shredifier: Slightly lowered fire rate, damage might be lower as well.
                      Fires slow moving bullets that explode on impact, making it able to deal dual-elemental damage.
                      Increases movement speed.
                      Fires in the shape of a circle, because circles have no start nor an end. (Infinity refference)

Infinity: Buffed damage and fire rate.
          Fires etech assault rifle bullets.
          Ricochets once and splits into 3 more projectiles on ricochet.
          Bullets penetrate targets (But it's ricochet projectiles do not)

(Limitless) Mongol: Lowered damage and fire rate.
                    Fires the same projectiles, but the child projectiles spread in a much thinner path.
                    Inaccurate.

(Apeiron) Lyuda / White Death / Lyudmilla:

Lowered damage by at least 30%.
Lowered fire rate.
Increased accuracy.
Better splitting; Splits into 4 more projectiles in a + shape and also splits earlier.
Slower projectile speed.

    #</Features>
