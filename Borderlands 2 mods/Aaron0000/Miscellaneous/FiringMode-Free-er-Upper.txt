				<category name="Frees up some somewhat unnecessary firing modes so that they can be used for other weapons, as well as adjusting said weapons (as needed) to be as close to their Unofficial Community Patch version as possible (expect some differences in things like tracers, or splash radius for most of them with splash). There are a few minor changes due to doing this;">
					<comment>Dahl blasters no longer fire in a triangular matter.</comment>
					<comment>Infection and Little Evie no longer arc.</comment>
					<comment>Retcher has a gentler arc. Also buffed the speed of it because it was just pitiful.</comment>
					<comment>Yellow Jacket no longer starts slow and becomes fast, moves at a constant 50% of a normal plasma caster. It also has increased base damage to accomodate the loss from the splash (was 70%, is 50% after changing the firing mode to a normal plasma caster's).</comment>
					<comment>Norfleet no longer fires out projectiles in a spread. The projectiles also no longer twist around in the air, and have marginally smaller splash. Weapon's accuracy was lowered to help it function roughly as it did before.</comment>
					<comment>Pitchfork bullets move faster. Can also no longer get multiple amp damage boosts.</comment>
					<comment>Sand Hawk can no longer get multiple amp damage boosts. It also starts out smaller than normal and gets larger as it flies away.</comment>
				</category>
