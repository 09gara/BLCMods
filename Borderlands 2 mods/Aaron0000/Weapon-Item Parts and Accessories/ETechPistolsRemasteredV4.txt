				<comment>PLASMA PISTOLS;</comment>
				<comment>Fires a Plasma projectile.</comment>
				<comment>Costs three ammo per shot.</comment>
				<comment>Dahl Plasma Pistols burst-fire three shots when not aiming-down-sights.</comment>
				<comment>Aiming down sights (for all Plasma Pistols) and firing will launch a powerful projectile that is multiple times stronger, but costs just as much ammo (Up to seven times for both).</comment>
				<comment>After firing a "charged" shot, you will be unable to fire the gun for a few seconds (Dahl variants have a shorter delay).</comment>
				<comment>Credit to Dave for letting me use his Singing Bird as inspiration for Plasma Pistols.</comment>
				<comment>DARTS:</comment>
				<comment>Projectiles now explode on impact with any enemy and have great homing capabilities (behaves like Wanderlust.)</comment>
				<comment>Increased projectile speed.</comment>
				<comment>Costs two ammo per shot.</comment>
				<comment>Dahl Darts burst-fire three shots when not aiming-down-sights, and five when aiming-down-sights.</comment>
				<comment>NOTES:</comment>
				<comment>Technically, the "Charged" shots are (up-to) seven projectiles simply fired so fast, that they register as one projectile upon hitting something (kinda like shotguns.) Therefore, if you happen to see a spray of projectiles when using less accurate versions of the weapon or Anarchy, don't be surprised.</comment>
				<comment>If using S&S Forever, there is a fix for it at the bottom.</comment>
