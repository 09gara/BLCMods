#<Quadtastic>

    #<Description>

        By Aaron0000

        When one's not enough, two is too few, and three has fleas, do more with four.

        Features;

        Turns every non-E-Tech barreled unique/legendary/seraph Shotgun in the game into quad-barreled versions. Enable the shotguns of your choice by scrolling down and replacing the # in "set" with an s. By default, the legendary shotguns are turned into quads.

        NOTES;

        If using the Classic Sledge mod, there is a compatibility fix for it located at the bottom of the Classic Sledge file.

        There is an option to turn the Unofficial Community Patch's Flayer into a Quad-Barrel, as well as a version for use with the Moonface mod's Flayer. Both are located at the bottom.

    #</Description>
