#<Slayer COMs>

    #<Description>

        By Aaron0000

            Turns a dozen skins in the game into "Slayer of Crawmerax" and "Slayer of the Seraphs" class mods. Crawmerax COMs boost Shock abilities and Capacity along with boosting six tier 4/5 skills (usually), and drop from Crawmerax. Seraph COMs boost Gun Damage, Damage-over-Time resistance, and regenerate ammo, while boosting five skills that help with slaying Raid bosses (of which they drop from all Seraph Guardians. Not including Dexiduous).
